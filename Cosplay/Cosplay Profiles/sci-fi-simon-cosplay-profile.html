I've always had an interest in cosplay and playing different characters. I was in college for performing arts for 2 years and loved it, especially making up characters and what clothes they wear. I watch a lot of videos on behind the scenes of films and such, I love seeing how the costumes are made so I decided to give it a go and I've never looked back!
My first cosplay was at the beginning of 2019 and it was my own futuristic design of my favourite Marvel character, The Punisher (which I've updated a little while ago). I like to call it Punisher 2077 because I'm super looking forward to Cyberpunk 2077! Out of the four I have, my favourite would have to be my Frank Helsing cosplay, which is a mix of The Punisher and Van Helsing.
The friends you make along the way in the cosplay community are friends for life. For a lot of people, cosplay is a way to be social and comfortable with themselves. Itâ€™s one of if the best confidence boosts you could ever receive and I'm talking from experience. I get asked if cosplay has to be an established known character. My answer is no, it can be any design you want it to be. It doesn't matter if your cosplay is bought or homemade. COSPLAY IS COSPLAY no matter what!
