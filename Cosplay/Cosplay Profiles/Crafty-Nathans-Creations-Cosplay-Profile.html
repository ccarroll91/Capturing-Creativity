"I got into Cosplay by accident, actually. I went to a convention one year and saw all the costumes and decided I could give it a go. Before I knew it, I was making massive costumes and one turned into two, two turned into three, and so on.

My first Cosplay was a plague doctor costume, complete with walking stick, treatises on the plague and leather aprons. My favourite cosplay so far has been my Nuka armour!


Name: Nathan

Location: Dublin

Characters: Nuka Cola Armour, WH Tech Priest (coming soon), Metro Exodus

My advice would be to take your time. So many people rush into things and bite off more than they can chew. It helps to take your time plan it out and take projects part by part. If you invest in that approach you cannot go wrong."
