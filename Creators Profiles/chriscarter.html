I had all the annuals that you get growing up, the Beano, the Dandy, at Christmas. I discovered the UK reprints of Marvel stuff and from there I’ve been hooked and someone needs to help me.
My book is being serialised pretty much like the Sonic the Comic or the UK anthology format in a book called Comic Anthology which is a magazine being run by Harry Gregson who is a former editor in chief at BigComics. It’s part of an anthology where there will be twelve ongoing stories, each told in chapters over six months.
My comic group started as a means of bullying each other to make comics, and it’s continued to be a way of bullying each other into making comics. Myself and my friends were going to be putting together our own anthologies and short comics collections anyway, and we thought that the easier way to do it would be if we pooled our resources, so that if one of us was at a show we could bring each other’s books and promote each other. Keep on talking to each other and make sure we were doing comics on a regular basis, and having someone standing over your shoulder definitely helps you to do that.
